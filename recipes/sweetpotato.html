Sweet Potato

Discription

This recipe will help you make the sweetest potatos.

Ingredients

  • pound of sweet potato
  • salt
  • oil

The steps

  1. Cut the potato in slices and have them cooked for 6min
  2. When done, let them rest for 5 min
  3. Fill a pot and preheat it. When oil starts boiling pour cooked potatos in them and fry until they get nice brownish color

Enjoy your sweet potatos!
