The Sauce

Discription

This recipe will help you make sauce for your hamburger and sweet potato.

Ingredients

  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1 tbsp of Vinegar
  • 1 tbsp of sugar
  • 11 tsp of heaped Mustard
  • 50g tomato ketchup
  • 1 head of onion
  • 1 teaspoon of Salt
  • 1 head of onion

The steps

  1. Whisk together the egg yolks, vinegar, mustard, and sugar for a few seconds.
  2. slowly, starting with a few drops, add the oil. After the first 20 ml, the rate of addition can be increased.
  3. Puree the ketchup with the finely chopped onion, the gherkin, the lemon juice, the parsley and possibly the garlic and whisk with the mayonnaise.

Enjoy your sweet potatos!
